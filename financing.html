Our Partners

Financing Providers

We work with three trusted healthcare financing partners to give you the most flexible options possible.

Cherry

Cherry is a patient financing platform designed to make healthcare affordable. With flexible payment plans and a quick approval process, Cherry lets you split the cost of your dental care into easy monthly payments.

  • Decisions in seconds
  • Flexible repayment terms
  • 0% APR options available
  • No hard credit pull to check eligibility
Apply with Cherry

CareCredit

CareCredit is a healthcare credit card designed specifically for medical and dental expenses. It allows patients to pay for treatments over time with promotional financing options accepted at thousands of providers.

  • Promotional 0% interest periods
  • Accepted at 260,000+ providers
  • Dedicated healthcare credit line
  • Flexible monthly minimums
Apply with CareCredit

HFD – Healthcare Finance Direct

HFD provides flexible in-house style financing for dental care with predictable monthly payment plans. HFD focuses on approval accessibility, making treatment more affordable for patients who may not qualify elsewhere.

  • High approval rates
  • Fixed monthly payments
  • No prepayment penalties
  • In-house style flexibility

Dental financing allows you to pay for your treatment in monthly installments rather than all at once. You apply through one of our financing partners (Cherry, CareCredit, or HFD), get approved quickly, and then begin your dental care. You repay the financing partner directly over your chosen term.
It depends on the provider. Cherry allows you to check your eligibility with only a soft credit inquiry — meaning no impact to your score until you formally accept an offer. CareCredit and HFD may perform a hard inquiry upon application. We recommend reviewing each provider's terms before applying.
Yes — all three of our financing partners allow early payoff without prepayment penalties. Paying off your balance early can also reduce the total interest you pay. Contact your provider directly to confirm the process and confirm any remaining balance.
The best option depends on your credit profile, how long you'd like to repay, and whether you want a promotional interest period. Cherry is great for quick approvals and soft credit checks. CareCredit offers longer promotional 0% APR periods. HFD is ideal if you've had difficulty qualifying elsewhere. Our front desk team is happy to walk you through your options.
Yes. Financing can be used for most dental services including routine cleanings, Invisalign, dental implants, crowns, veneers, and emergency care. The treatment cost just needs to meet the financing provider's minimum amount. Talk to our team for specifics about your treatment plan.
