Our Story

Spring Valley Dental Associates was founded in 1994 with a simple mission: to provide exceptional dental care in a comfortable, welcoming environment. What started as a single-doctor practice has grown into a comprehensive dental team serving the Dallas community with advanced technology and time-tested techniques.

Our practice philosophy centers on conservative, preventive care that helps patients maintain their natural teeth for life. We believe in taking the time to educate our patients about their oral health and working together to develop treatment plans that fit both their clinical needs and personal preferences.

Today, we're proud to be a trusted partner in oral health for families throughout Dallas, from routine cleanings to complex restorative work, all delivered with the personal attention and gentle care that has defined our practice for over two decades.
