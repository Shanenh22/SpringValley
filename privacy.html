Privacy Policy

We rely on the following legal bases for processing your information: your consent (for contact forms and communications), our legitimate interests (site operations, security, and analytics), and legal obligations.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted service providers who help us operate our website and practice, including:

  • Website hosting providers
  • Google Analytics (for website analytics)
  • Email and communication service providers

These service providers process data on our behalf under appropriate safeguards. We may also share information when required by law.

Data retention

We retain website-related information for as long as needed for the purposes described above, unless a longer period is required or permitted by law. Contact form submissions are typically retained for 2 years unless you request deletion.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. No system is completely secure; please take care when transmitting information online.

Children's privacy

Our website is intended for general audiences and not directed to children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

Your choices & rights

  • Google Analytics: Use the opt-out browser add-on linked above.
  • Access/Deletion: You may request access to or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at info@springvalleydentistry.com.

California residents
