Types of Oral Surgery We Offer

Our dentists perform a range of minor oral surgical procedures right here in our office. These include simple and surgical tooth extractions, biopsy of suspicious oral lesions, frenectomies and bone grafting related to dental implants. We coordinate with specialists for more complex procedures to ensure you receive the best care.

Comfort & Sedation

Your comfort is our priority. We use local anaesthesia to numb the area completely and offer oral conscious sedation for patients who feel anxious. Our gentle approach and clear communication help you stay relaxed and informed from start to finish.

What to Expect During Recovery

Recovery times vary depending on the type of surgery, but most patients experience some swelling and mild discomfort that resolves within a few days. We'll provide you with detailed aftercare instructions, including diet recommendations, oral hygiene tips and signs to watch for. Following our guidance will help you heal quickly and prevent complications.
