Why Choose Dental Implants for Tooth Replacement

Dental implants are the gold standard for replacing missing teeth because they fuse with your jawbone, providing a stable foundation that preserves bone health and prevents surrounding teeth from shifting. A complete implant consists of three parts: a threaded titanium post that integrates with your bone, an abutment that connects the post to a restoration, and a custom crown, bridge or denture that restores your smile.

Implants are made from biocompatible materials and are designed to last for decades with proper care. They look and feel like natural teeth, allowing you to smile, chew and speak with confidence.

Our Dallas Dental Implant Process

Your implant journey begins with a thorough consultation and 3D imaging to plan your treatment. The process typically involves several phases:

1. Consultation & Planning

Thorough examination and 3D imaging to plan your treatment for optimal results.

2. Implant Placement

Small titanium post is surgically placed into your jawbone during a comfortable procedure.

3. Healing & Integration

3-9 months for bone to grow around the post in a process called osseointegration.

4. Abutment Placement

Once healed, we attach a connector called an abutment to support your restoration.

5. Final Restoration

Custom crown, bridge or denture is secured to restore function and appearance.

Throughout the process, we prioritise your comfort and provide detailed aftercare instructions to ensure the best possible outcome.

When Are Dental Implants Needed?

Implants may be recommended when a tooth is missing due to various causes. Understanding when implants are the best solution helps ensure optimal treatment outcomes.

Tooth Loss Causes

  • Severe cavities beyond repair
  • Cracked or fractured roots
  • Advanced gum disease
  • Dental trauma or injury
  • Congenital missing teeth (anodontia)

Preventive Placement

  • After planned extractions
  • To prevent bone loss
  • Maintain facial structure
  • Prevent teeth shifting
  • Preserve jaw alignment

Replacement Options

  • Single tooth implants
  • Multiple tooth bridges
  • Full arch restorations
  • Implant-supported dentures
  • All-on-4 solutions
